PSN maintains a Pool of Employees who work when there is a requirement by PSN customers like Amazon, Flipkart etc. The customer or PSN does this by opening slots/ shifts as per their requirement, the notification of which is sent to the Pool Employee on his registered App. The Pool Employee applies for these Slots as per hi convenience. The Attendance of the Employee is captured on the Slot day and his salary for the day is calculated based on the rate card assigned to his state and designation. On-Time App - Attendance Confirmation Software

PSN Supply Chain Pvt Ltd

Mobile App and Web Development, Deployment, Implementation, Training, Maintenance and Support. Server setup for development and testing
Technology: Flutter, Dart, Django Python, MySQL, Cloud: AWS EC2

Employees of PSN who are outsourced, Provide a daily confirmation to the outsourced Agency regarding their attendance next day based on the Roster generated for them through a Mobile App. This helps the Agency to assess the strength that will be available for work the next day. Confirmation and Attendance trend Reports are generated by the application based on this data. These Reports help PSN to assess people who do not provide confirmation, who provide confirmation but do not attend or do not comply to the time etc. The Application also sends notices to regular defaulters on the App. The Application also captures the usage of the Mobile App alerts or notification by each user.

Background: The Deonar dumping ground (DDG) has been facing several incidents of fire and other rampages in the recent past. The management has brought in better security and firefighting arrangements into the site by installing surveillance booths, skilled security and firefighting personnel and latest equipment to aid in the surveillance and firefighting activities. The details of the equipment, personnel and the daily surveillance activity report, incidents are currently maintained in registers. The maintenance and distribution of the data is cumbersome and requires a lot of physical and manual interventions.
Need for the new system: The new system has to maintain the data and document in a digital form and make it easily accessible for the stakeholders.
Features: A standalone application at the DDG to maintain the staff and equipment records, daily surveillance plans and records, fire drill plans and records and the SOPs. A web based application to make available daily, periodic reports, SOP and other documents view, SOP maintenance and management with version control, Incidence management and dashboards of activities to the various stakeholders of the DDG management.
