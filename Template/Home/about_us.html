History

We began in Gorai, Borivali, managing data entry projects for EC Bank, MCGM Estate Department, and Octroi. Our focus on high-quality, accurate, and timely solutions quickly built our reputation in data management.

-2003

In 2004, we acquired our first office at Orbit Industrial Estate, Malad, and grew our team to 100+ professionals. We expanded into hardware supply and maintenance, offering comprehensive IT infrastructure solutions to optimize performance and ensure smooth operations.

-2004

In November 2016, we launched our Software Division in Malad, expanding into custom software development. By January 2017, we provided specialized software, hardware support, and data entry services for CIDCO, offering seamless end-to-end solutions.

-2008

In December 2018, we secured the Gram Panchayat Development and Planning project, integrated with a fellowship program under Mr. Devendra Fadnavis, highlighting our expertise in managing large-scale government initiatives and local governance.

-2016

Post-Covid, we maintained seamless support and launched innovative solutions like the Knowledge Resource Center with SVT College. We also developed the CM Apprenticeship Management System for GHRDC, Goa, and gained recognition with multiple HRMS projects, strengthening our HR management presence.

-2018

In 2021, we expanded into educational software, creating solutions for institutions like Government Law College, Mumbai. We also developed an Audit Utility for CGST Thane and a Citizen Scheme Portal for Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation, delivering secure, compliant digital solutions. Additionally, we ventured into Labour Law Compliance, helping businesses stay updated with regulations.

-2021

In 2023, we expanded our services in enterprise IT solutions, cloud computing, and data security, focusing on scalable, secure, and high-performance applications for the public sector. Our expertise in IT and business process automation helps clients streamline operations and achieve measurable results.

-2023

In 2024, we aim to expand into new markets, focusing on AI, cloud infrastructure, and data analytics. We're committed to driving growth, innovation, and digital transformation for businesses and government organizations with cutting-edge technologies, while ensuring quality, security, and continuous improvement.

-2024
