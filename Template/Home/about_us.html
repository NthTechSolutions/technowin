History

We've rented a small office in Gorai – Borivali to deliver data entry projects for EC Bank, MCGM – Estate Department, and Octroi.

-2003

We purchased our inaugural office in Malad - Orbit Industrial Estate, expanded to 100+ staff, and began providing hardware supply and maintenance.

-2004

Expanding data entry projects to various Government Bodies, securing our first AMC contract with MCGM – Octroi. Our team grew to 400+.

-2008

Started Software Division in Malad on Nov 14, 2016. By Jan 2017, developed software, handled hardware, and performed data entry for CIDCO.

-2016

In Dec 2018, we secured 'Gram Panchayat Development and Planning' project integrated with a fellowship program under Mr. Devendra Fadnavis.

-2018

Survived post-Covid with customer support. Developed 'Knowledge Resource Center' with SVT College. Created CM Apprenticeship Management System for GHRDC, Goa. Awarded HRMS projects.

-2021

Established educational software for Government Law College, Mumbai, Audit Utility for CGST Thane, Citizen Scheme Portal for Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation. Ventured into Labour Law Compliance

-2023

Continuing ..

-2024

Technowin IT Infra Pvt. Ltd.
